SOCIAL EXCLUSION

What is social exclusion??

When a group of people ignores a person and excludes them from the group, it is called social exlcusion. It can make a person feel left out and lonely.

Why does social exclusion happen?

Happens mostly because of the group not wanting to be with person or someone convinced the group to ignore them.

How stop social exclusion?

If you are a part of the group, make sure the person feeling left out not feel left out. Make sure no one is alone.

In conclusion, only you can stop social exclusion, mainly because you can decide whether to ignore a person or not
