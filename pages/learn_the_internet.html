"Learn or get to know what the internet is"

Learning about the internet is crucial for several factors and reasons. If we don't understand the internet industry well, there are higher chances that you may get attacked, scammed, and many more. It is important to know what the internet looks before trying to try it out. Here are 3 things you should know about the internet industry.

Three ways to help you learn about the internet industry

Social media platforms

Firstly we have here, is called "Social media platforms". Everywhere you go, you may always hear a word named Social media and yes, social media is a massive place where you can explore many catergories and many more. It can be fun yet it can be vicious to use it. There are various groups of people out there on the internet so, look out and becareful.

Innappropriate ADS and websites

Secondly we have here, is called "Innappropriate ADS and websites". Let's just imagine that you have found something to watch on the internet, suddenly, an ad popped up. But little did we know, it might be a scam or something Innappropriate. At this case, you must ignore them as much as you can, because it can be scam or it might be something concerning with cyberbullying. So, be aware of those kinds of ADs.

Online communities

Lastly we have here, is called "Online communities". As we might have known, there are various groups of people on the internet. People who are influencers, who are scammers, hackers, who are here to promote things, who are here to let other people down and many more. So, it is much safer if you know which kinds of people belongs into which kinds of diversities.

Not only these factors, there are many things out there that are concerning with a word called "Internet". Which is why we consider learn the internet to be apart of our preventions for cyberbullying. Get to know about the internet first, before you use it!
