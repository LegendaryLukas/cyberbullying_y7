VERBAL BULLYING

What is verbal bullying??

Verbal Bullying is when a person uses words to harm other people by sending hurtful, unhelpful and mean messages. These messages can include Threats (Such as blackmailing)and Insults. This type of bullying can keep a person's mind occupied and lower their self esteem.

Why does verbal bullying Happen?

Verbal bullying often happens due to the bully wanting to harm the other person but doe not want to use violence. So they use words as a way to harm people. Words are sometimes more hurtful than violence as it can leave a bad memory in the victim's mind.

How stop Verbal Bullying

We cannot force people to not be bullies. If you get a hate text online, the best option is to block or ignore them. And if possible, report them.

To conclude, Verbal bullying is bad for anyone who sees or hears it. It often happens because the bully want to harm the victm and you should ignore them if it happens.
