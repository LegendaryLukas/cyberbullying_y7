Impersonation

What is impersonation?

Impersonation is when a person pretends to be another person to trick people in believing that it is the true person. When they have fully gain the people's trust, they start doing things that the real person does not do to ruin the victim's reputation.

Why does Impersonation Happen?

Same as false rumours, when the bully does not have actual data to ruin a person's life so they pretend to be that person to destroy the victim's life.

How to stop Impersonation?

We can by making sure that the person we are talking to is the real person. You will notice something different when someone is an imposter. If you do find an imposter, best thing is to block them and tell everyone about it.

To conclude, Impersonation is a very sneaky way of bullying that makes it harder to find the real culprit.
