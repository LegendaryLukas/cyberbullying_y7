"Focus on yourself as much as possible"

Focusing on yourself more and giving yourself more space and time will also help you maintain a healthier life as well as considering it as a way of ignoring and avoiding yourself from these bullies. Do not let them take down on you, love and take a good care of yourself!

Three ways to help you focus on yourself more

Do your favorite activities

For the frist tip we have here, is called "Do you favorite activities". Let us consider this as a way of ignoring and avoiding the bully, let you,yourself think what you want to do in your life, what you really want to achieve and many more. If you put yourself first, there is nothing to worry about. Do not give yourself a full attention to those bullies on online. You will get through it! For example, as for me I like to go on trips a lot so that is one thing I personally love to do!

Invest in self-improvement

For the second tip we have here, is called "Invest self-improvement". Let and take yourself onto an adventure of how to learn and grow, whether if it is by going on solo trips, reading books or many more. This is also a way of keeping yourself distracted by the bully on online. So, instead of wasting your precious times, invest yourself to help you develop new skills, gain new perspectives, and become the best version of yourself.

Limit distractions

For the last third tip we have here, is called "Limit distractions". Identify any distractions or sources of negativity in your life and try to minimize them as much as possible. For example, trying to ignore any bullies that are trying to attack you on online and many more, instead of worrying yourself on these stupid things, try to focus on yourself! As for me, I love spending times with my love ones, for example, my beyong loving mother! She has always helped me feel better, made me feel like I am special and many more!

Focusing on ourselves can be hard time to time. But if you practice it and do the job, then I believe that you got this! Try to fill up your surroundings with bright positive energy.
