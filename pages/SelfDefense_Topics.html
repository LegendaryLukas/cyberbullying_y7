Preventions for cyberbullying

Here are some preventions that might be helpful for cyberbullying!

Try to remain calm

I know it is hard for some people to remain calm right after they heard bad tragic words from people as well as ignoring them but here are some tips to help you avoid them

Learn the internet

It is extremely important to learn the internet because there are many things that have been occuring through many social media platforms. If you don't know the flow of the internet, you might have some issues

Learn more

Set your account to private and use VPN

Sometimes, there are people called "Doxxers" out there who wants to collect your private informations and many more. The best way to prevent from these kinds of things are to use VPN or private your account.

Learn more

Ignore the messages if unnecessary

We all know that some of the people on online loves to criticize you, spread false rumours and let yourself down by trying to use different ways and methods on online. The best way to avoid these are to simply ignore them.

Learn more

Focus on yourself

6 out of 10 people might be facing "social exclusion" on online. Due to that matter, people tends to be depressed, sad, self-conciousness and many more. The best way to prevent you is to focus on yourself rather than the others.

Learn more

Stay alert and try to report them

Some of us might have already known or heard about the digital Impersonation. It is a thing that we might need to be aware of and the best way to be aware of this kind of thing is to stay alert and if the impersonater has gone way too far then, report them.
