"Stay alert and try to report them if it gets worse"

If things get more complicated and hard to handle, try to report them and stay even more alert before it could get any worse. Here are some of the tips that might be useful to help you even more and so on. So, becareful my dears!

Three things you can do to report and stay alert from them

Report and ask help

For the frist tip we have here, is called "report them and ask help". When you’re actually targetted by them or if anything worse is going to happen, do not hesitate, immediately try to ask help or report through social media and many more. Most importantly, do not be frightened or scared, because, in each and every single bully's perspective, they want their targetted people to be scared. So, do not stay still and start focusing on things you should be doing.

Try to log out of the specific account

For the second tip we have here, is called "Try to log out of the specific account". It is important to log out of the account before it gets any worse since later on, you never know what is going to happen. Try to make a new account and try to not respond them as much as possible.

Seek social support

For the last third tip we have here, is also called "Seek social support". Even though some of us might say the fact that we are not soft, we can comfort ourselves, we can support ourselves and many more, at the end, whenever something big or small happens, we need some comfort from someone, we need some emotional support. So, do not be scared, find your people to tell these things to. It is never too late.

In our lives, there is at least one time where we will have to go through a hard time, lose your love ones, an unnecessary mental breakdowns and many more. But don't worry, you are not alone. This is just the meaning of life! In the end, you will always be proud of yourself for what you have gone through!
