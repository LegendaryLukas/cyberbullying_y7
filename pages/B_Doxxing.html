DOXXING

What is doxxing??

Doxxing is basically when a person's private or important infomation is exposed. These information can include your location,

Why does doxxing Happen?

Doxxing happens because the bully thinks its funny or want to threathen the victim of something.

How to stop doxxing?

Most of the time, when a Troll appears the best thing to do is stop using your computer or phone and wait until the troll is gone because ignoring would not work. If you are using a messaging app and a troll starts annoying people, block the troll and carry on.

Try to avoid Doxxing as much as possible. Report people doxxing information.
