False Rumours

What is false rumours?

When a person spreads fake or unreal news about someone that is bad, it is called false rumours. These rumours and aimed to ruin a person's reputation or damage their social life. Sometimes the people believe the rumours and avoid or humiliated the person because of it.

Why does false rumours happen?

It happens when the bully want to ruin the victim's life but dont have actual information about the person so the bully makes up fake information to convince people that the victim is this or that.

How to stop false rumours?

False rumours can be stopped by making sure that the information you are getting is real and you are not being lied to.

In conclusion, false rumours can have long-lasting effects on a person's health and life. Be sure that the information you are getting is true and make sure there is evidence to support it
