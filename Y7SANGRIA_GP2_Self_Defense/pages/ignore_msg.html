"Ignore the messages that might hurt or harm you"

When it comes to bullies, the main reason why they want to do these kinds of things is to see how you would respond, if you respond them by showing your emotions being scared, then it'll give them more motivation and possibilities to try to bully you more. If you do not express any emotions, there is a higher chance that it will make them furious, more over, they'll move on.

Three ways to help you ignore the messages

Try to block them

For the frist tip we have here, is called "Block them". When they are trying to attack you from online platforms, try to block their accounts as much as possible before anything bad happens. It will also help to reduce more chances of you, receiving negetive things such as hate comments, Innappropriate posts and many more.

Try to not respond

For the second tip we have here, is called "Do not respond". Try to ignore, avoid, not communicate and express any types of emotions towards them. As I have mention before, if you try to express your emotions, it will get worse, if you try to not express your emotions, they will eventually move on. Pretty simple and easy.

Change your status into offline

For the last third tip we have here, is called "change your status into offline". Changing your status into offline mode will help you ignore those bullies and make them more confused, since your profile is offline, they will not be able to know whether if you are here with them or not. If this tip doesn't work, try to quit social media for a few days, as if a way of ignoring them.

It will be hard for some people to directly just ignore them but let's think of this way, if you really want to not get into these kinds of stupid situations, try your best to avoid them as much as you can!
